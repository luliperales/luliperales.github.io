See what all the fuss is about

Stream is an app designed for those who like to record their lives through video. For celebrities, this can be the perfect social media to connect more closely with your fans. For politicians, a new way of reaching the community. Anyone and everyone is welcome to record their livestreams and broadcast them through our app. Additionally, follow the people who interest you and comment on their livestreams as well. To make sure that you catch your friends at the perfect timing for their Streams, make sure to turn on the push notifications!

Features

  • Get push notifications when your favorite celebrities are live-streaming.
  • Write comments and ask questions that Streamers can answer on the spot.
  • View your monthly and yearly Stream entries.
  • Switch easily between Friends, Global, or Celeb feeds.

Requirements

  • Stream requires iOS 3.0 or higher on an iPhone6 or iPhone6 Plus.
